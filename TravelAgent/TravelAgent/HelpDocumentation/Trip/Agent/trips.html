Trips Help

Understanding "Trips" Page

On "Trips" page, you can browse all available trips users can purchase or reserve. On this page you can add new trips and edit or delete existing ones.

  • 1. Departure Date and Time: This field represents the exact date and time when your trip is scheduled to begin. It is similar to the departure time for a flight or train, where it is important to know when to be at the airport or station.
  • 2. Arrival Date and Time: This field indicates the exact date and time when your trip is expected to end. It can be the time when you arrive at your destination or when you are expected to return from the trip. For example, if you are traveling by plane, this could be the time of your scheduled landing at the airport.
  • 3. Departure Location: This field represents the place from where your trip starts. It could be the name of a city, airport, station, or any other specific address that identifies the location of departure. For example, it can be "Belgrade" or "Nikola Tesla Airport".
  • 4. Arrival Location: This field indicates the destination of your trip. It can be the name of a city, airport, station, or any other specific address that identifies the location where you are traveling to. For example, it can be "Paris" or "Charles de Gaulle Airport".

Add

Clicking the plus selected on the picture opens a new menu where you can create a new trip.

Departure and destination

Clicking on either the departure or destination input field will put it into focus, double clicking on the map will select the desired destination and set it as current for either departure or destination depending on which field is in focus.


Restaurants

The left box shows all of the selected restaurants for our trip, the right box shows all restaurants, in order to select a restaurant you can simply drag it from the right box to the left one or press ctrl + left-arrow-key.


Tourist attractions

Tourist attractions are places tourist can visit, places that have some interesting history, fun activities, good foods...


Accomodations

Accomodations are places where tourists will reside during the trip, there can be multiple accomodations as the tourists might not be staying in one place the whole time.


Finalize

Here you can set the departure date and time, arrival date and time as well as the price of the trip, after all of the previous forms have been filled properly, you can click the finish button to create the trip. If you want to change any of the previously set things, you can always go back with the back button in the top left.


Edit

First you need to click on the trip you want to edit, after that you can click on the highlighted button and you will start the editing process. The process is the same as for the trip creation, the only difference is, all of the things are already selected, you can remove certain things, or add new ones. For a detailed explanation on each tab, go read Add


Remove

First you need to click on the trip you want to remove, after that you can click on the highlighted button which will ask you are you sure you want to delete this, if you click yes, the selected trip will be permanently deleted.
